Slurm User Group Meeting 2022

The Slurm User Group Meeting (SLUG'22) this fall will be held online once again.

In lieu of an in-person meeting, SchedMD will broadcast a set of presentations on Tuesday, September 20th, 2022 through YouTube Live from the SchedMD Slurm channel. All of the sessions are listed under the Slurm User Group 2022 playlist. No registration is required.

Viewers are encouraged to ask questions through the live chat feature. Community presentations are pre-recorded, but the presenters have been encouraged to participate and respond directly through the live chat if able. For the SchedMD presentations (which will be done live), SchedMD staff will be moderating the chat, and will forward relevant and timely questions to the presenter. The last 5-10 minutes of each presentation will be reserved for further Q+A, as well as provide a brief break between each session.

Please note that each presentation is contained in a separate broadcast, and you will need to switch between them at the breaks.
